About Our Hostel

P.G.Men's Hostel was inaugurated by Professor R.C.Mehrotra, the then Vice-Chancellor on 24.10.1975 with one hundred single bedded rooms. During the last few years, it has not only grown in times of physical assets such as a library with a capacity of sixty seats, a computer lab, all are air-conditioned and working for twenty hours a day, a decent modern gym, a laundry room with five washing machines, solar water geysers, a newspaper room, a magazine room, a small canteen, a photocopy outlet, two badminton courts, but also in respect of intellectual property and human resource...

Admission Process

  • Students must be enrolled in a full-time course at Delhi University
  • Fill out the online application form during the admission period
  • Submit required documents including college admission letter, residence proof, and income certificate
  • Merit-based selection process considering academic performance and distance from hometown
  • Selected candidates must complete fee payment within specified timeline
